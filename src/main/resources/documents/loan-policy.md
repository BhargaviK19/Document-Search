# Loan Policy Document

## Personal Loans
The maximum personal loan amount is $50,000.
Minimum loan amount is $1,000.
Loan tenure ranges from 12 to 60 months.
Interest rate for personal loans is 8.5% per annum.

## Home Loans
The maximum home loan amount is $500,000.
Minimum down payment required is 20% of property value.
Loan tenure for home loans ranges from 5 to 30 years.
Interest rate for home loans is 6.5% per annum.

## Eligibility Criteria
Minimum credit score required is 650.
Minimum monthly income required is $3,000.
Maximum debt to income ratio allowed is 43%.
Applicants must be at least 21 years of age.

## Loan Processing
Loan approval takes 2 to 5 business days.
Documents required include pay stubs, bank statements, and tax returns.
Co-applicants are allowed for home loans only.
