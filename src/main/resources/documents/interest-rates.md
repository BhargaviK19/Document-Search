# Interest Rates Policy

## Personal Loan Rates
Standard personal loan rate is 8.5% per annum.
Premium customers with credit score above 750 qualify for 7.0% per annum.
High risk customers with credit score below 600 are charged 12.5% per annum.
Fixed rate loans are available for 12 to 36 month tenures.
Variable rate loans are available for tenures above 36 months.

## Home Loan Rates
Standard home loan rate is 6.5% per annum.
First time home buyers qualify for 5.9% per annum.
Investment property loans are charged 7.2% per annum.
Rates are fixed for first 5 years then become variable.

## Business Loan Rates
Small business loans start at 9.5% per annum.
Established businesses with 3+ years history qualify for 8.0% per annum.
Startup loans are charged 11.0% per annum.
Maximum business loan tenure is 10 years.

## Rate Review
Interest rates are reviewed quarterly.
Rate changes take effect from the first day of the following month.
Customers are notified 30 days before any rate change.
