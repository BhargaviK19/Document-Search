# Compliance and Regulatory Rules

## KYC Requirements
All customers must provide government issued photo ID.
Proof of address required — utility bill or bank statement within 3 months.
Income verification required for loans above $10,000.
KYC must be renewed every 2 years for active customers.

## AML Policy
Transactions above $10,000 must be reported to FinCEN.
Suspicious activity reports must be filed within 30 days.
Customer source of funds must be verified for deposits above $25,000.
PEP customers require enhanced due diligence.

## Data Privacy
Customer data is retained for 7 years after account closure.
Data sharing with third parties requires explicit customer consent.
Customers have the right to request data deletion after account closure.
All customer data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

## Audit Requirements
All loan decisions must be logged with reasoning for 5 years.
System access logs must be maintained for 2 years.
Annual compliance audit is mandatory for all product lines.
Regulatory reports must be submitted quarterly to applicable authorities.
