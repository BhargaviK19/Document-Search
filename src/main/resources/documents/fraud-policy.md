# Fraud Detection Policy

## Transaction Monitoring
All transactions above $10,000 are automatically flagged for review.
Transactions from new devices in different countries trigger immediate hold.
More than 3 failed login attempts results in account lockout for 24 hours.
Unusual spending patterns compared to 90-day history trigger fraud alerts.

## Fraud Indicators
Multiple transactions in different cities within 1 hour are flagged.
Card not present transactions above $5,000 require additional verification.
First time international transactions require SMS verification.
Transactions at unusual hours between 2am and 5am are monitored closely.

## Fraud Response
Confirmed fraud cases are reported to authorities within 24 hours.
Customer is notified immediately via SMS and email when fraud is detected.
Compromised cards are cancelled and replaced within 3 business days.
Fraud investigation takes 5 to 10 business days to complete.

## Customer Liability
Customer liability for unauthorized transactions is capped at $50.
Zero liability applies if fraud is reported within 2 business days.
Customer must report suspicious activity within 60 days of statement.
